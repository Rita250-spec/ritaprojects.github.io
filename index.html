Analysing Employee behaviour using SQL

NextGen Corp. is a growing technology business that develops unique software and hardware solutions. The organisation prioritises hiring top talent and maintaining high employee satisfaction to promote growth. However, there is growing concern over personnel turnover, performance variability, and wage discrepancies within departments. At the end of the analysis, we gave recommendations to NextGen Corp, advising them to leverage long-serving employees for mentorship, understand and scale what keeps them engaged, and plan for succession in other to enhance the retention and performance of its employees.

Crust & Crumb Bakery Co. Five Year Financial Model 2025

Crust & Crumb Bakery Co. is a start up Bakery Business. The objective of the Five Year Financial Model is to build a three statement financial model and conduct a business valuation. Secondly, to seek for investment to fund the start up of the business. Thirdly, to demonstrate the business potentials, showing its financial projection and growth potentials. The Financial Model also presents three scenerios; Best case, Base case and Worse case. The Bakery Business operates in the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods Sector. The Model under the Base Scenerio shows a projected Equity Investment of 129,690,848 Naira, Enterprise Value of 8,912,473,553 Naira and Equity Valuation of 7,724,547,454 Naira.
